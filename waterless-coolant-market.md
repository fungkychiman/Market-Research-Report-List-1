Propylene glycol, on the other hand, is a safer alternative, often used in food and pharmaceuticals, making it more suitable for applications requiring non-toxicity. Both types provide enhanced protection against corrosion and evaporation while optimizing vehicle performance and durability over traditional water-based coolants.</p></p>
