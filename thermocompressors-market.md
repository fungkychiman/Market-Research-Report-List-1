Single nozzle thermocompressors utilize one inlet to compress steam for applications requiring moderate pressure and flow. They are typically simpler and more cost-effective for specific tasks. In contrast, multi nozzle thermocompressors feature multiple inlets, allowing for greater efficiency and adaptation to varied operational requirements. This versatility makes them suitable for complex systems and higher demands, appealing to industries needing enhanced performance and greater control over steam management.</p></p>
