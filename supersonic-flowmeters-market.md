<p><p>Supersonic flowmeters are specialized devices used to measure fluid flow rates at high velocities. The market for these flowmeters is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Inline Flowmeters** are installed directly within the pipeline, offering precise measurements and minimal pressure loss.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Clamp-On Flowmeters** attach externally to pipes, allowing for easy installation without disrupting the flow or pipeline integrity.</p><p>3. **Insertion Flowmeters** are partially inserted into the pipeline, providing a balance between accuracy and ease of installation. Each type caters to specific application needs and operational environments.</p></p>
