In contrast, cloud-based solutions are hosted on remote servers, allowing for easier access, scalability, and lower upfront costs. They enable real-time data sharing and collaboration among healthcare professionals while reducing the burden of maintenance and updates. Both options cater to different organizational needs and preferences.</p></p>
