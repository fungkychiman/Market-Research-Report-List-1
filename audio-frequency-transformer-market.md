<p><strong>The Audio Frequency Transformer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Input Transformer</li><li>Output Transformer</li><li>Interstage Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The audio frequency transformer market includes three main types: input transformers, output transformers, and interstage transformers. Input transformers are used to match the impedance of microphones or other sources to the amplifier, enhancing signal quality. Output transformers connect amplifiers to speakers, ensuring efficient power transfer and sound fidelity. Interstage transformers facilitate signal transfer between different amplifier stages, providing isolation and preventing noise. Together, these components enhance audio performance in various applications, including recording studios, live sound systems, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
