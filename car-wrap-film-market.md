<p><strong>The Car Wrap Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cast Film</li><li>Calendered Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car wrap film market is primarily divided into two types: cast film and calendered film. Cast film is produced by pouring molten PVC onto a casting surface, resulting in a thinner, more flexible material with excellent clarity and conformability, making it ideal for complex curves. In contrast, calendered film is manufactured by passing PVC through rollers, producing a thicker and more durable material suitable for flat surfaces and simpler designs. Both types cater to different application needs and aesthetic preferences.</p></p>
