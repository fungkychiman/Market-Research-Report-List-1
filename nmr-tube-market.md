<p><p>The NMR tube market encompasses various types designed for specific applications in nuclear magnetic resonance spectroscopy. The primary types include quartz and borosilicate glass tubes. Quartz NMR tubes are favored for their superior thermal stability and minimal background noise, making them ideal for high-resolution spectroscopy. Borosilicate glass tubes, known for their affordability and durability, are commonly used in routine applications. Each type serves distinct needs in research and analytical laboratories, influencing their adoption based on performance and cost considerations.</p></p>
