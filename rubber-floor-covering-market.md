<p><ul><li>Homogeneity Rubber Floor Covering</li><li>Heterogeneous Rubber Floor Covering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rubber floor covering market features two primary types: homogeneous and heterogeneous rubber floor coverings. Homogeneous rubber floor covering consists of a single layer with uniform composition, offering consistent performance and durability, ideal for high-traffic areas. In contrast, heterogeneous rubber floor covering is made of multiple layers with varying materials, providing enhanced design options and improved acoustic properties. Each type caters to different aesthetic and functional needs, making them suitable for diverse applications, including commercial, residential, and industrial environments.</p></p>
