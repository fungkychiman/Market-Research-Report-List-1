<p><p>The 3D printer filaments market features various types catering to diverse applications. HIPS (High Impact Polystyrene) is ideal for support structures due to its dissolvable properties. PVA (Polyvinyl Alcohol) serves as another dissolvable support material, particularly in multi-material printing. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) and its variant PETG (Glycol-Modified PET) are popular for their strength and clarity. Metal filaments combine metal powders with a polymer matrix, enabling intricate metal part printing. Other filaments include nylon, TPU, and specialty composites, broadening the market's versatility.</p></p>
