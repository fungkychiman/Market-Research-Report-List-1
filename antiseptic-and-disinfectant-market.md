<p><p>The antiseptic and disinfectant market comprises various types, each serving unique purposes. Quaternary ammonium compounds are non-toxic and effective against a broad spectrum of pathogens, making them popular for surface sanitation. Chlorine compounds are potent germicides widely used in water treatment and surface disinfection. Alcohols, particularly ethanol and isopropanol, rapidly kill bacteria and are common in hand sanitizers. Aldehyde products, like formaldehyde, offer strong antimicrobial properties. Enzymes break down organic matter to enhance cleaning, while "others" include alternatives like essential oils and peracetic acid.</p></p>
