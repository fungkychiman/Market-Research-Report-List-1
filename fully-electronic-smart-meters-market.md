DC meters are designed for applications involving battery storage systems and renewable energy sources, measuring direct current flow efficiently. Conversely, AC meters cater to conventional power grids, accurately tracking alternating current usage in residential, commercial, and industrial settings. Both types contribute to enhanced energy management, enabling real-time monitoring, data analytics, and improved billing processes, thus supporting the transition to smarter energy systems.</p></p>
