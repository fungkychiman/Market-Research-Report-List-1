<p><ul><li>Monocular Stereo Vision Sensor</li><li>Binocular Stereo Vision Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stereo vision sensor market is primarily categorized into monocular and binocular types. Monocular stereo vision sensors utilize a single camera to capture disparity through motion or depth estimation, making them more compact and cost-effective for certain applications. In contrast, binocular stereo vision sensors use two cameras positioned apart to mimic human vision, enabling precise depth perception and 3D mapping. This makes binocular systems preferred for tasks requiring high accuracy, such as robotics, autonomous vehicles, and advanced manufacturing.</p></p>
