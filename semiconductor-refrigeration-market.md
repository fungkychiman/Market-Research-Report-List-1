<p><p>The semiconductor refrigeration market can be categorized into single-stage and multi-stage systems. Single-stage systems utilize a single thermoelectric module for cooling and are typically suited for compact applications with limited temperature differentials. In contrast, multi-stage systems employ multiple thermoelectric modules stacked together to achieve greater temperature reductions, making them ideal for specialized applications requiring more significant cooling capabilities. Both types serve various industries, including electronics, automotive, and medical, by providing efficient and compact cooling solutions.</p></p>
