<p><strong>The Environmental Due Diligence Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Desk Study</li><li>Site Investigation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Environmental Due Diligence Services encompass two primary market types: Desk Study and Site Investigation. Desk Studies involve reviewing existing documents, historical site data, and regulatory records to assess potential environmental liabilities and risks. In contrast, Site Investigations entail on-the-ground assessments, including soil and water testing, to identify contaminants and evaluate conditions. Both approaches are critical in ensuring informed decision-making during property transactions, compliance with regulations, and minimizing environmental risks for businesses and stakeholders involved.</p></p>
