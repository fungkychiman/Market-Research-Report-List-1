<p><p>Digital impression systems are categorized into two main types: standalone systems and portable systems. Standalone systems are typically stationary devices that provide high-resolution impressions and are often used in dental or orthodontic offices. They offer advanced scanning capabilities but require a dedicated space. In contrast, portable systems are compact and designed for on-the-go use, allowing practitioners to capture digital impressions in various locations. These systems enhance flexibility and convenience, making them suitable for home visits or remote care applications.</p></p>
