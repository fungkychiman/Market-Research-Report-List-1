<p><p>The medical X-ray generator market is categorized into high-frequency and low-frequency X-ray generators. High-frequency X-ray generators produce a steady and consistent X-ray output, resulting in improved image quality, reduced exposure time, and minimized patient radiation dose. They are commonly used in advanced diagnostic imaging. Low-frequency X-ray generators, on the other hand, provide a more variable X-ray output, which can lead to lower image quality and higher radiation exposure, making them suitable for basic imaging applications.</p></p>
