<p><p>The inorganic waterproof coating market is segmented into two primary types: liquid and dry. Liquid coatings are typically applied using brushes, rollers, or spray, offering ease of application and a uniform finish. These coatings cure to form a durable, waterproof barrier on various surfaces. Conversely, dry coatings refer to pre-applied, solid forms, such as sheets or films, that are adhered to surfaces for waterproofing. Both types are designed to protect structures from moisture penetration and environmental damage.</p></p>
