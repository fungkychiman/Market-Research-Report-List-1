<p><p>Styrene Maleic Anhydride (SMA) copolymers are versatile materials used in various applications due to their unique properties. The market is primarily divided into two types: Alternate Styrene-Maleic Anhydride Copolymer and Random Styrene-Maleic Anhydride Copolymer. Alternate SMA copolymers feature a regular arrangement of styrene and maleic anhydride units, enhancing mechanical strength and thermal stability. In contrast, Random SMA copolymers have a more random distribution of these units, offering improved compatibility with different polymers, making them suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>
