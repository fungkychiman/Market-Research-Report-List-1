<p><p>Diaphragm coupling in turbo machinery connects shafts while allowing for misalignment and absorbing vibration. Single diaphragm couplings utilize one diaphragm to transmit torque, ideal for moderate loads. Double diaphragm couplings, featuring two diaphragms, enhance load capacity and flexibility, suitable for more demanding applications. Multiple diaphragm couplings incorporate several diaphragms for maximum torque transmission and compensation for significant misalignments. Each type is designed to optimize performance, enhance reliability, and minimize maintenance in various turbo machinery setups.</p></p>
