<p><ul><li>Cellular</li><li>Wi-Fi</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Firmware Over-The-Air (FOTA) market is primarily categorized into two types: Cellular and Wi-Fi. Cellular FOTA utilizes mobile networks to deliver firmware updates, ensuring vehicle connectivity even in remote areas. It offers broad coverage and convenience but may incur data costs. In contrast, Wi-Fi FOTA relies on wireless internet connections, typically available at home or public hotspots, allowing for larger updates without cellular data charges. Each type provides distinct advantages based on connectivity, cost, and accessibility.</p></p>
