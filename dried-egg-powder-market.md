<p><p>The dried egg powder market comprises three main types: whole egg powder, egg white powder, and egg yolk powder. Whole egg powder contains all components of the egg, retaining both flavor and nutrition, making it versatile for various applications. Egg white powder focuses on the protein content, ideal for health-conscious consumers and baking. Egg yolk powder is rich in fats and emulsifiers, used predominantly in sauces and dressings. Together, these products enhance convenience and extend the shelf life of traditional eggs.</p></p>
