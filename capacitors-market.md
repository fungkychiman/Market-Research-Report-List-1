<p><p>The capacitors market comprises various types, each suited for specific applications. Class X and Class Y capacitors are used primarily for safety and EMI suppression in power circuits. Ceramic capacitors, known for their reliability, are widely used in consumer electronics. Film capacitors offer stability and low distortion, making them ideal for audio equipment. Electrolytic capacitors provide high capacitance for power supply filtering, while supercapacitors excel in energy storage applications. Other types include tantalum, mica, and paper capacitors, catering to niche demands.</p></p>
