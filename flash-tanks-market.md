<p><p>Flash tanks are categorized into vertical and horizontal types based on their design and function in separation processes. Vertical flash tanks are optimized for space efficiency, utilizing a tall, narrow structure to facilitate efficient vapor-liquid separation, making them suitable for limited floor area. In contrast, horizontal flash tanks provide a larger surface area, enhancing phase separation and handling larger fluid volumes better. Both types are essential in industries like oil and gas, where efficient separation processes are critical.</p></p>
