<p><p>The aqua vaccines market is segmented into three main types: injectable vaccines, dip vaccines, and oral vaccines. Injectable vaccines are administered through injections, offering targeted immunity to aquatic animals. Dip vaccines involve immersing fish in a vaccine solution, providing rapid immune response through skin absorption. Oral vaccines are delivered through feed, allowing convenient vaccination while ensuring consistent intake by the animals. Each type addresses specific needs in aquaculture, enhancing disease prevention and improving overall fish health.</p></p>
