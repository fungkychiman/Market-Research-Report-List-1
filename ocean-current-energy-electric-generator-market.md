<p><strong>The Ocean Current Energy Electric Generator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal Shaft Generator</li><li>Vertical Shaft Generator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ocean Current Energy Electric Generator market is divided primarily into two types: Horizontal Shaft Generators and Vertical Shaft Generators. Horizontal Shaft Generators align parallel to the ocean floor, efficiently capturing lateral currents, making them suitable for high-speed tidal flows. In contrast, Vertical Shaft Generators are oriented perpendicular to the water flow, allowing them to harness the energy from both tidal and ocean currents effectively, often resulting in larger rotor designs. Both types aim to convert kinetic energy into electricity sustainably.</p></p>
