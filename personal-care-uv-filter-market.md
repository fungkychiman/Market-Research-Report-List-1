<p><p>The Personal Care UV Filter market comprises two main types: organic and inorganic UV filters. Organic UV filters, often chemical compounds, absorb UV radiation and are favored for their lightweight feel and broad-spectrum protection. In contrast, inorganic UV filters, primarily mineral-based like zinc oxide and titanium dioxide, reflect UV rays and are known for their skin-friendly properties, making them ideal for sensitive skin. Both types are essential in formulating sunscreens and other personal care products to protect against harmful UV exposure.</p></p>
