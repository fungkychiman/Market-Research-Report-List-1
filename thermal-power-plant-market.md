<p><p>Thermal power plants encompass various types based on their energy sources and efficiency. Biomass or co-fired power stations utilize organic materials alongside fossil fuels, promoting sustainability. Combined cycle power plants enhance efficiency by using both gas and steam turbines. Combined heat and power systems produce electricity and useful heat simultaneously, maximizing energy use. Fossil-fuel power plants, the most traditional, rely solely on coal, oil, or natural gas, raising concerns about emissions and environmental impacts. Each type plays a distinct role in energy production.</p></p>
