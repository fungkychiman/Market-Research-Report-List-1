<p><ul><li>LED Backlit</li><li>CCFL</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The large format displays market encompasses various technologies, primarily LED backlit and CCFL (Cold Cathode Fluorescent Lamp) displays. LED backlit displays utilize light-emitting diodes to enhance brightness and color accuracy while being energy-efficient and thinner than traditional displays. In contrast, CCFL displays, which use fluorescent tubes for backlighting, offer good color reproduction but are bulkier and less energy-efficient. The choice between these technologies often depends on application needs, budget, and desired display performance characteristics.</p></p>
