<p><p>The motorcycle pipes heat shield market comprises primarily two types: header heat shields and exhaust pipe heat shields. Header heat shields are designed to cover the engine's exhaust headers, minimizing heat dissipation and protecting surrounding components. Exhaust pipe heat shields, on the other hand, protect the body and legs of the rider from excessive heat generated by the exhaust system. Both types enhance rider safety, improve performance, and contribute to the overall efficiency of the motorcycle's thermal management system.</p></p>
