<p><p>The Water Ecological Monitoring market encompasses various types, primarily focusing on water quality monitoring and biomonitoring. Water quality monitoring involves assessing physical, chemical, and biological parameters to ensure safe and clean water sources. This includes testing for pollutants, temperature, pH, and dissolved oxygen levels. Biomonitoring, on the other hand, studies the effects of environmental changes on aquatic organisms, utilizing their responses as indicators of ecosystem health. Both sectors are essential for managing water resources and sustaining ecological balance.</p></p>
