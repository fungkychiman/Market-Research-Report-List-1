<p><p>The medical disposable isolation gowns market features two primary types: full-back isolation gowns and open-back isolation gowns. Full-back isolation gowns provide comprehensive coverage and protection, making them ideal for procedures requiring high levels of fluid resistance. Conversely, open-back isolation gowns facilitate ease of wear and removal, often used in lower-risk environments or where repeated donning and doffing are necessary. Both types serve critical roles in infection control during medical procedures, ensuring safety for healthcare workers and patients.</p></p>
