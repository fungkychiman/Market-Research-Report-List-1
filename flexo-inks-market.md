<p><ul><li>Solvent-based Printing Inks</li><li>Water-based Printing Inks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flexo Inks market primarily includes solvent-based and water-based printing inks. Solvent-based inks offer fast drying times and high adhesion on various substrates, making them ideal for flexible packaging and labels. However, they emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs), raising environmental concerns. In contrast, water-based inks are more eco-friendly, utilizing water as a solvent, which reduces harmful emissions. They are suitable for various applications, including food packaging, but may have slower drying times and lower performance on non-porous surfaces.</p></p>
