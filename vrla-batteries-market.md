<p><ul><li>Absorbed Glass Mat Battery</li><li>Gel Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VRLA (Valve Regulated Lead Acid) battery market comprises two main types: Absorbed Glass Mat (AGM) batteries and Gel batteries. AGM batteries utilize a fiberglass mat to absorb the electrolyte, providing efficient power delivery and durability, making them ideal for high-drain applications. Gel batteries, on the other hand, use a thickened electrolyte in a gel form, offering enhanced safety and resistance to deep discharge. Both types are maintenance-free and commonly used in renewable energy systems, UPS, and various transportation applications.</p></p>
